How It Works

Our process is simple: You securely upload your parcel shipping data via SFTP (or via API Integration) and send us your carrier contract information. We run this data through our proprietary Vaire Rating & GPT Analysis Engine, which automatically audits, rates, and interprets your shipments. You receive clean, accurate audit results with actionable insights — delivered via Power BI dashboards or directly by email.

How we help your bottom line

We use AI to detect billing anomalies and reduce your shipping expenses.
